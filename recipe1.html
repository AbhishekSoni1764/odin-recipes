BELOW ARE THE INSTRUCTIONS GIVEN BY GOOGLE WHICH ARE THE STANDARD WAYS TO MAKE TEA

  1. Fill up the kettle with water.
  2. Boil the kettle.
  3. Place a teabag in your favourite mug.
  4. Pour boiling water into your favourite mug.
  5. Brew the tea for a few moments.
  6. Remove and dispose of the teabag.
  7. Add milk.
  8. Add sugar.

BELOW ARE THE STEPS TO MAKE A DESI TEA

  1. Fill up the patila with little water(20ml)
  2. Add one tablespoon tea and boil
  3. Smash some Adrak and Elichi and add the mixture in boiling water.
  4. Let it Rest some time(30 sec)
  5. Fill Your mug with milk and add in the kettle
  6. Let the mixture boil at low flame.
  7. After 7-8 min use a chalni to pour the tea in your mug.
  8. Enjoy One of the Best Tea of Your Life
