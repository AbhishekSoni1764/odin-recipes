HERE IS THE STANDARD RECIPE TO MAKE ALOO PARATHA

  1. Boil a potato in cooker.
  2. Now mash that boiled potato and add spices( chilli , onions , salt , coriander , red chilli) and Keep it aside
  3. Take some flour and mix it while adding little water to make dough
  4. Make small mould in that dough and add the potato mix in between it and cover it
  5. Now using a chakli belan flaten the dought in a round shape while adding some dry flour to not let it stick
  6. Put some butter in a pan/tava and cook the paratha from both sides until golden brown.
  7. Garnish and serve with butter and Curd.
