  1. Break noodles into bowl, add flavour sachet.
  2. over 250ml cold water, stir and heat in microwave oven on High for 2 1/2 min (850 watt).
  3. Enjoy!

HERE'S THE BEST WAY TO MAKE MAGGI

  1. Add approx 1 cup water in a kadhai
  2. Add the Magic Mix in water and boil it
  3. Add some salt and red chilli acc to your taste
  4. When water starts to evaporate add maggi
  5. Cover it and let it cook for 4-5 min
  6. Enjoy with chips or Kurkure
